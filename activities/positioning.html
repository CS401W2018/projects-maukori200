What is normal flow in HTML?

What does float do? Why do you need to clear the float?

float make elements floated and and you need to clear the float in order to removed it from the normal document flow.

What do absolute, fixed, and relative do?

absolute can allows elements to be placed on top of each other, fixed can takes elements out of the normal flow of page and put it in a separate layer and relative can allows objects to be placed in relation to one another.
