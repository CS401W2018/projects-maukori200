Different Destinations

  • Costa Rica is a rugged, rainforested central american country with coastlines on the Caribbean and Pacific. Costa Rica
  • Kiribati is an island country in the Micronesia subregion of Oceania in the central Pacific Ocean. It permanent population is over 119,000 and more than half live in Tarawa atoll. Kiribati
  • New Zealand is an island country in the southwestern Pacific ocean. It consists of two main landmasses- the north island and the South Island. NZ
