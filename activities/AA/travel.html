Eco-Friendly Travel Tips

  • Pack reusable bags, containers and utensils to avoid using single-use plastics
  • Support local farmers by eating at restaurants that serve locally sourced, seasonal food.
  • Bontique hotels are often smaller and more sustainable than large chain hotels.
  • Support local economies by buying locally-made products.
  • Use public transportation, cycle, or walk whenever possible.
