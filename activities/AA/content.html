Packing for a Green Trip

Travel light and reduce waste with these smart packing strategies. Choose reusable items and eco-conscious gear.

  • Bring a Refillable Water Bottle
  • Use Solid Toiletries Sampoo bars and toothpaste tablets reduce plastic waste.
  • Bring a Reusable shopping bag It is useful for groceries, shopping materials.
  • Pack multi use Clothing Choose clothes that layer well and work for multiple activities like fewer items = lighter bag
